Game Rules

Players take turns to roll then select a scoring combo of dice. The points can be saved and the turn passed, or the player can roll the remaining dice again. If a bust is rolled all points from the round are lost.

Single 1s are worth 100 each and single 5s are worth 50 each. Three dice of any number are worth their number times 100 (e.g. 5+5+5 = 500). If more than three of a number are rolled, the score is doubled for each addition to the set.
